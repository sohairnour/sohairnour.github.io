Impressive wearables that will change your life for the better

What’s the first wearable? We’d say the first calculator watch from Sony in 1975. The first modern wearable would be the first wireless headset thanks to the discovery of Bluetooth in 2002. Do fitness trackers make you healthier? No – but they do give you key insight and data to help and encourage you to make informed decisions. Are wearables mostly for sports? There’s actually a wearable for just about everything – check out our Wearables category to see them all! Innovation has always propelled us to new standards of technology. Less than a decade ago, a fitness tracker simply counted your steps or monitored your heart rate. Now, they can pay for your meal at a restaurant. Wearable technology has become the norm and has even transcended our wrists to make our lives better and easier. Here are 10 impressive wearables you’ve got to see.

New Wearable Tech Captures Dance Movements Transforms Them Into Visual Sensations

When we think of wearable technology, we don’t see ballet shoes as a wearable device. But in fact, E-Traces pointe shoes designed by Lesia Trubat, may be the world’s first wearable ballet shoes. The shoes capture the movement and pressure of the dancer’s feet using Lilypad Arduino technology and transmits this information to a smartphone via Bluetooth.
